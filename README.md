## Yann Tanguay

I have worked professionally in leadership roles within non-profit organisations and the public sector, including crisis response services at CAMH—Canada’s largest mental health research hospital—and as an officer within the Canadian Armed Forces Reserves.  I have experience coordinating teams, supporting performance management processes, and contributing to operational planning and quality assurance within high-stakes environments where decisions often must be made with incomplete information.

I have an undergraduate degree in Economics and am presently an MBA Candidate.  Both of which I credit for cultivating an affinity for quantitative methods and the practical use of data in improving efficiency and supporting more informed and better decision-making.

The projects shared on this profile are independent analyses in which I apply analytical tools and concepts acquired through my education and professional experience.  

I am particularly interested in analytics as a means of story-telling; identifying patterns in data, and exploring relationships through regression and modelling, and increasingly leveraging AI-assisted approaches, to develop insights that support leadership decision-making within real organisations.
